Pursuing Creative Passions

Nick grew up in the central state of Hesse Germany, just north of Frankfurt. While visiting Berlin on November 9th 1989, he experienced the fall of the Berlin Wall first-hand.

In 2003, he graduted with a Bachelor of Science in the Music Recording Industry program at Middle Tennessee State University, and went on to produce and engineer songs for many songwriters and bands. He also wrote, produced, and engineered original music for 2 films which were entered into film festivals in Nashville TN and Atlanta GA.

Nick has always had a passion for technology and how it can push the boudaries of knowledge and human experience. He has coded as a hobby for the past 8 years while working in the IT field at a Credit Union. Just recently, he has enrolled in the Nashville Software School to persue this passion professionally. In his free time, he is an avid painter, and just recently opened his first online store, Ephemeral Wave. He enjoys hiking, playing football, and spending time with his young son and wife.
